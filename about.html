About Me

Welcome, Here's a little insight into who I am!

My Journey

It all started as a self-taught learner, driven by curiosity for Python and automation. I quickly realized you could replace time-consuming manual processes with pipelines that run on their own. That curiosity led me to Jedha, where I worked on real-world cases: data cleaning, modeling, ETL/ELT pipelines, visualization. I came out with an RNCP Level 6 certification and solid hands-on experience with the modern data stack.

Experience

My data experience was built on real cases, not just training. The most significant mission was a 6-month remote project for 360°, based in Yonezawa, Japan. A connection made during a trip, turned into a data mission: modernizing their traveler data collection and analysis strategy, building an automated Python pipeline and a self-service Streamlit dashboard, delivering 3 actionable buyer personas to guide the city's marketing campaigns.

Alongside that, I develop portfolio projects that reflect my technical interests: an airline data warehouse modeled with dbt, an automated ETL pipeline via GitHub Actions analyzing forecast reliability across 250+ cities, and an analytics pipeline on SQL Server built around hotel booking data.

At Jedha, as a Teaching Assistant, I have supported more than 50 learners since January 2025 on Python, SQL, dbt and Power BI: code review, debugging, sharing engineering best practices, guidance on real business cases. Explaining concepts to someone discovering data forces you to truly master them yourself.


Goals & Aspirations

My goal is to join a team where data is a real driver of decision-making, and where I can contribute to high-impact projects. I remain committed to continuous learning, particularly around AWS and pipeline orchestration, and to sharing knowledge within the data community.
