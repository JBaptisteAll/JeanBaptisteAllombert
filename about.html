About Me

Welcome, Here's a little insight into who I am!

Hi, I’m Jean-Baptiste, a Data Analyst with 20 years of experience in the hospitality and tourism industry. Leveraging this background, I combine business expertise with strong technical skills in Python, SQL, Power BI, and Dataiku to deliver insights that drive performance, optimize decision-making, and uncover growth opportunities.

In my free time, I love exploring nature, especially hiking in the mountains. Curious and self-driven, I seek to thrive in a dynamic environment where I can continue learning and delivering value.


My Journey

My data journey started through self-learning with platforms like Coursera, where I completed courses from Google and Meta. To deepen my expertise, I joined an intensive Data Analytics Bootcamp at Jedha, where I worked on real-world datasets, focusing on data cleaning, visualization, and machine learning. This hands-on experience sharpened my technical skills and strengthened my problem-solving mindset.

Teacher Assistant Experience

After graduating, I was invited to stay on as a Teacher Assistant at Jedha. In this role, I supported new cohorts by:

  • Guiding workshops and coding sessions.
  • Assisting with Python, SQL, and data visualization.
  • Reviewing and giving feedback on student projects.
  • Helping debug code and clarify complex concepts.
This role not only consolidated my knowledge but also enhanced my communication and mentoring abilities.


Goals & Aspirations

I aspire to contribute to impactful projects where data can drive meaningful change — from supporting sustainable initiatives to enabling smarter services and innovative solutions. My objective is to combine strong analytical expertise with strategic thinking to deliver insights that create tangible value. At the same time, I remain committed to continuous learning and to sharing knowledge within the data community.
